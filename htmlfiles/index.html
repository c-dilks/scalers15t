Spin Patterns [PDF]

  • Blue and yellow beam bXing patterns are shown for two fills per page. Vertical axis is STAR bXing and horizontal axis is the spin. Red means like-spin bXing and green means unlike-spin bXing; cyan means bXing with empty bunch(es).
  • This year we have 24-bunch patterns subdivided into 3 8-bunch subpatterns
    • subpattern [1]: + - + - - + - +
    • subpattern [2]: - + - + + - + -
    • subpattern [3]: + + - - + + - -
    • subpattern [4]: - - + + - - + +
  • Spin pattern numbers: logs what spin patterns were collided for each fill; the line "@ STAR" is for the spin pattern seen at STAR bXings and the line "@ CDEV" is the pattern quoted on CDEV
    • So far, we collide a pattern from Group A with a pattern from group B (using CDEV patterns here)
    • Group A: [111] [222] [122] [211]
    • Group B: [333] [444]
